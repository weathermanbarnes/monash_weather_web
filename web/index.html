The Monash University weather web portal has been created by the School of Earth, Atmosphere and Environment in collaboration with the School of Mathemathics. It provides a suite of observations and forecasts from various sources for research and academic purposes. The sources of all information housed here are cited on each product.

The various products and information housed on this site are generated from external sources. The quality and timeliness is therefore not the responsibility of the administrators of this site. All information housed here should be used with caution.
